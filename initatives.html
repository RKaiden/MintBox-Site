Programs & Partnerships

Hudson Guild
Partnership

In collaboration with Hudson Guild, a community center in Chelsea, NYC, we foster sustainable food practices. We donate custom-designed sub-irrigation watering systems to Hudson Guild. This intiative educates our community about gardening and sustainability whilst providing access to freshly-grown produce from radishes, herbs, & more.

2023-Present

Learn More

Avenues School
Partnership

MintBox EDU aims at improving education surrounding healthy eating and sustainability. Utilizing our Baby Sprout Gardening Kit, we host workshops and donate kits to local schools. Providing a gardening chassis, seeds, soil, and our instruction manual, we guide students in elementary and middle school on the process of growing their own herbs.

2021-Present

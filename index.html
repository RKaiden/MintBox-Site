We are a registered 503(C) non profit founded in 2019 by four 8th graders during the pandemic aimed at improving community wellness through gardening.

Our Story Recent Initiatves

We aim to promote very healthy eating through gardening and sustainability. Our recent initiatives include our partnership with Hudson Guild. We work to donate custom-designed gardening solutions and lead class curricula aimed to promote wellness and healty eating.

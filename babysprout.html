Our Story

As our first project, Baby Sprout was designed to introduce a easy-to-use gardening kit to the market. Taking some time off of school in Avenues's Mastery program a dedicated project workspace, we were able to design the chassis and packaging for the Baby Sprout Kit along with the instruction manual to provide a beginner with everything needed to get started in gardening.

What's inside?

Our Baby Sprout kit contains a laser-cut chassis, seeds of your choice (including chives, basil, mint, oregano, parsley, and chives), packaged soil, and our Baby Sprout Gardening Guide for beginnners. Through our all-in-one gardening kit, users will be able to develop their own mini herb garden in no time.

June 2021

Laser cut process of our first kits

In June of 2021 we developed our first Baby Sprout Gardening Kits. Each kits in the intial 25 batch were laser-cut individually and glued together by our team of product engineers and packaged to our kits format.

First stall at Union Square Park

After selling out our initial banch we developed a 2.0 version of Baby Sprout that focused on the ceramic chassis. We sold this chassis in a 75-kit run at Union Square Park to passerbys in the summer and online.
